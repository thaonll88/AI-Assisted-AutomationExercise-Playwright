# Submit a Product Review

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/product_details/{id}  
**Flow:** Write and submit a review for a product on its detail page.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com/products`.
2. Click **"View Product"** on any product card.
3. Scroll down to the **"Write Your Review"** section on the product detail page.
4. Fill in the review form:
   - **Your Name** — enter your name.
   - **Email Address** — enter a valid email address.
   - **Add Review Here** — enter the review text.
5. Click the **"Submit"** button.
6. Verify the success message is displayed: **"Thank you for your review."**
7. Verify the review form fields are cleared after approximately 2 seconds.

## Expected Result

- Review is submitted without errors.
- "Thank you for your review." message appears.
- Form is reset after submission.
