# Register New User

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/login  
**Flow:** Create a brand-new user account from the Signup section.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com/login`.
2. Locate the **"New User Signup!"** section on the right side of the page.
3. Enter a name in the **Name** field.
4. Enter a unique email address in the **Email Address** field.
5. Click the **Signup** button.
6. You are redirected to the **"Enter Account Information"** page (`/signup`).
7. Select a **Title** radio button: `Mr` or `Mrs`.
8. The **Name** and **Email** fields are pre-filled — verify they are correct.
9. Enter a **Password**.
10. Select **Date of Birth**: Day, Month, and Year from the dropdowns.
11. Optionally check **"Sign up for our newsletter!"**.
12. Optionally check **"Receive special offers from our partners!"**.
13. Fill in the address section:
    - First Name
    - Last Name
    - Company (optional)
    - Address (Address 1)
    - Address 2 (optional)
    - Country (dropdown)
    - State
    - City
    - Zipcode
    - Mobile Number
14. Click the **Create Account** button.
15. Verify the **"Account Created!"** page is displayed with a success message.
16. Click the **Continue** button.
17. Verify the logged-in username appears in the top navigation bar as **"Logged in as [name]"**.

## Expected Result

- Account is created successfully.
- User is automatically logged in and redirected to the homepage.
- Navigation bar shows **"Logged in as [name]"**, **Delete Account**, and **Logout** links.
