# Login with Valid Credentials

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/login  
**Flow:** Log in to an existing account using correct email and password.

## Preconditions

- A registered user account exists with known email and password.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com/login`.
2. Locate the **"Login to your account"** section on the left side of the page.
3. Enter the registered **Email Address**.
4. Enter the correct **Password**.
5. Click the **Login** button.
6. Verify the user is redirected to the homepage.
7. Verify the navigation bar shows **"Logged in as [name]"**.

## Expected Result

- Login succeeds.
- User lands on the homepage.
- Nav bar displays **"Logged in as [name]"** along with **Logout** and **Delete Account** links.
