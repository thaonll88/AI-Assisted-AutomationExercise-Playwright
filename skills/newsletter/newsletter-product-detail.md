# Newsletter Subscription on Product Detail Page

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/product_details/{id}  
**Flow:** Subscribe to the newsletter using the subscription widget on a product detail page.

## Steps

1. Navigate to any product detail page, e.g., `https://www.automationexercise.com/product_details/1`.
2. Scroll down to the **footer** or subscription section at the bottom of the page.
3. Locate the **"Subscription"** section.
4. Enter a valid email address in the subscription input field.
5. Click the **Subscribe** (arrow/submit) button.
6. Verify the success message: **"You have been successfully subscribed!"**

## Expected Result

- Newsletter subscription works consistently across product detail pages.
- Success confirmation message is displayed after subscribing.
