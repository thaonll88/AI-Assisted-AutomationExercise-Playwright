# Newsletter Subscription on Homepage

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com  
**Flow:** Subscribe to the newsletter using the subscription widget on the homepage.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com`.
2. Scroll down to the **footer** or subscription section near the bottom of the page.
3. Locate the **"Subscription"** section with the heading **"Get the most recent updates from our site and be updated yourself"** (or similar).
4. Enter a valid email address in the subscription input field.
5. Click the **Subscribe** (arrow/submit) button.
6. Verify the success message is displayed: **"You have been successfully subscribed!"**

## Expected Result

- Email is accepted and subscription is confirmed.
- Success message appears in or near the subscription widget.
