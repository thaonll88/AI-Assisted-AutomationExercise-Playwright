# Newsletter Subscription on Cart Page

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/view_cart  
**Flow:** Subscribe to the newsletter using the subscription widget on the cart page.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com/view_cart`.
2. Scroll down to the **footer** or subscription section near the bottom of the page.
3. Locate the **"Subscription"** section.
4. Enter a valid email address in the subscription email input field.
5. Click the **Subscribe** (arrow/submit) button.
6. Verify the success message is displayed: **"You have been successfully subscribed!"**

## Expected Result

- The subscription widget works on the cart page the same as on the homepage.
- Success message confirms the subscription.
