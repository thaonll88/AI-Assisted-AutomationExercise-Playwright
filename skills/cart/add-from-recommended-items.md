# Add Product from Recommended Items

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com  
**Flow:** Add a product to the cart using the Recommended Items carousel on the homepage.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com`.
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the homepage.
3. Locate the **"Recommended Items"** carousel section.
4. Browse through the recommended product cards (use carousel arrows if needed).
5. Click the **"Add to cart"** button on a recommended product.
6. Verify the confirmation modal appears: **"Your product has been added to cart!"**
7. Click **"View Cart"** in the modal.
8. On the cart page (`/view_cart`), verify the recommended product is listed with correct name, price, and quantity of 1.

## Expected Result

- Product from the recommended items section is successfully added to the cart.
- Cart page shows the correct product details.
