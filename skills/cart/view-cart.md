# View Cart

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/view_cart  
**Flow:** Navigate to the cart page and verify the cart contents and layout.

## Steps

1. Add at least one product to the cart (from the listing or detail page).
2. Click the **Cart** link in the top navigation bar, or click **"View Cart"** from the add-to-cart modal.
3. Verify the URL is `https://www.automationexercise.com/view_cart`.
4. Verify the cart table is displayed with the following columns:
   - **Item** (product image + name)
   - **Description** (product name)
   - **Price** (unit price in Rs.)
   - **Quantity** (editable or displayed)
   - **Total** (price × quantity)
   - Delete (✕ icon per row)
5. Verify the **"Proceed To Checkout"** button is visible.
6. Verify the grand total or per-item totals are displayed.

## Expected Result

- All added products appear in the cart table with correct details.
- Proceed to Checkout button is accessible.
