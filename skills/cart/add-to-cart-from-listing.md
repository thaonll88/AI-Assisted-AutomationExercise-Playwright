# Add to Cart from Product Listing

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/products  
**Flow:** Add a product to the cart directly from the products listing page.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com/products`.
2. Hover over a product card to reveal the **"Add to cart"** button.
3. Click the **"Add to cart"** button on the desired product.
4. Verify a modal/notification appears with the message: **"Your product has been added to cart!"**
5. The modal offers two options:
   - **"Continue Shopping"** — closes the modal and stays on the products page.
   - **"View Cart"** — navigates to the cart page (`/view_cart`).
6. Click **"Continue Shopping"** or **"View Cart"** as needed.
7. If "View Cart" was clicked, verify the product appears in the cart table.

## Expected Result

- Product is added to the cart.
- Confirmation modal is displayed.
- Cart icon/link in the nav reflects the updated item count.
