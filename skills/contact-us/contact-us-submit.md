# Submit Contact Us Form

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/contact_us  
**Flow:** Fill and submit the contact form.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com/contact_us`.
2. Verify the **"Get In Touch"** heading is visible.
3. Fill in the contact form fields:
   - **Name** — enter your full name.
   - **Email** — enter a valid email address.
   - **Subject** — enter the subject of the enquiry.
   - **Message** — enter the message body.
4. Optionally attach a file using the **"Upload file"** input.
5. Click the **"Submit"** button.
6. A browser confirmation dialog appears: **"Press OK to proceed!"**
7. Click **OK** to confirm submission.
8. Verify the success message is displayed: **"Success! Your details have been submitted successfully."**

## Expected Result

- Form is submitted after confirming the browser dialog.
- Success message replaces the form on the page.
- A **"Home"** button appears to navigate back to the homepage.
