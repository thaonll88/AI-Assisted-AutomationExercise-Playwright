# View All Products

**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/products  
**Flow:** Navigate to the products listing page and verify all products are displayed.

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com`.
2. Click the **Products** link in the top navigation bar.
3. Verify the URL changes to `https://www.automationexercise.com/products`.
4. Verify the page heading **"All Products"** is visible.
5. Verify a grid of product cards is displayed. Each card should contain:
   - Product image
   - Product name
   - Price (in Rs.)
   - **"Add to cart"** button
   - **"View Product"** link
6. Verify the left sidebar shows **Category** filters (Women, Men, Kids) and **Brand** filters.

## Expected Result

- Products page loads successfully.
- 32+ product cards are visible in the grid.
- Sidebar filters are visible and interactive.
