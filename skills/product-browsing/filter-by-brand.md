**URL:** https://www.automationexercise.com/products

## Steps

1. Navigate to `https://www.automationexercise.com/products`.
2. Locate the **Brands** panel in the left sidebar (below the Category panel).
3. Click a brand name (e.g., **Polo**).
4. Verify the page navigates to the brand URL (e.g., `/brand_products/Polo`).
5. Verify the page heading shows the selected brand (e.g., **"Brand - Polo Products"**).
6. Verify only products belonging to that brand are displayed.
7. Verify the product count matches the expected number listed in the sidebar.

## Expected Result

- Product grid shows only items from the selected brand.
- Page heading confirms the active brand filter.
